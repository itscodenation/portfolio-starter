About me

Sandra Opakunle

Born in Nigeria but raised in Chicago Illinois. Youngest of four children and she strayed away from the family path of going into the medical field to find something she was interested in. Attended Whitney M. Young Magnet High School where she was introducted to Computer science which was an graduction requirement. She took it her sophomore year because she was scared but when she did, she loved it. She joined Girls Who code, did the Summer Immersion Program with JP Morgan Chase bank, and she joined Genesys Works which gave her the opportunity to learn swift and work with a porfessional company coding. During her Senior year she completed her internship at TransUnion which gave her hands on experience working and creating with developers. She was very passionate about coding that she went on to participate in the Google CSSI program, Code Nation, and many more programs because she was very determined to learn as much as possible.
